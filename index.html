PERSON OF THE WEEK

Caleb Porzio (@calebporzio) is a full-stack developer from Buffalo, NY, who has been building Laravel apps for the last five years and Vue apps for the past three. He’s an active contributor to the Laravel framework and shares his knowledge as a speaker and writer. To help developers build rich, dynamic Laravel apps without all the complexity, Caleb created the Livewire framework. He’s also the creator of AlpineJS, a framework for composing JavaScript behavior in your markup, and co-host of the podcast No Plans to Merge. Thank you for sharing and caring, dear Caleb!
